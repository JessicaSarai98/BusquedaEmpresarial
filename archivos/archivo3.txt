The Mexican Olympic Committee confirmed the death of Ernesto Canto, winner of gold at the 1984 Los Angeles Olympics Games (1959-2020).
The Mexican Olympic Family mourns the death of Ernesto Canto, Member of the Executive Committee of COM and an icon of the world march;
He won everything and was consecrated with the gold obtained in the 20 km of the JO Los Angeles 84. We wish his loved ones prompt 
resignation. RIP. Among his elite track records, Canto was enshrined in the world championship in 1983, which gave him the pass 
to the highest glory of the Mexican Olympism.
Canto, who was fighting against liver and pancreatic cancer, passed away at the age of 61 and will be remembered as one of the 
best athletes in the history of Mexico.
